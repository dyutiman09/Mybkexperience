What is MyBKExperience Survey?

MyBKExperience Survey is an online customer feedback program. Burger King relies on honest customer feedback from its customers. This MyBK Experience survey will tell you how satisfied the people are who have visited one of your restaurants. Get rewarded for completing the survey online at www.mybkexperience.com. You will receive a free Whopper or voucher code that you can redeem at your nearby Burger King restaurant. This survey is available 24/7 online at mybkexperience.com and takes approximately 4 to 5 minutes.

It only takes a few seconds to take the survey and your receipt from Burger King, and in return the company will offer you a coupon code to redeem on your next visit.

What are mybkexperience survey rules?

It is best to final read the rules set by Burger King before participating in their online survey program, MyBKExperience. The following therefore lists the rules that you must observe when taking part in the MyBKExperience customer survey.

  • The minimum age is 18 years. Be a Legal U.S. Citizen.
  • You cannot participate if you are a Burger King employee or a direct relative of a fast food chain employee.
  • To take the survey, you’ll need a real Burger King receipt.
  • One receipt corresponds to one entry.
  • The premium period varies between 230 days, depending on the premium.
  • To redeem the voucher code, you need the receipt that you used in the survey.

What are mybkexperience survey rewards?

After submitting your feedback, Burger King will reward you with a voucher code. If you have the code, you can redeem it in the last restaurant you visited and receive the following meals:

  • Original Chicken Sandwich
  • Croissan’s Wich
  • Whopper Sandwich
  • Free Chicken or Veg Whopper
